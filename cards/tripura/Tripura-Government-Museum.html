Tripura Government Museum

West Tripura district
The Tripura Government Museum, also known as Tripura State Museum, is housed in the historic Ujjayanta Palace in Agartala, West Tripura district. Established in 1970 and relocated to the palace in 2013, it is Northeast India’s largest museum, featuring 22 galleries with sculptures, terracotta, coins, textiles, paintings, and tribal artifacts showcasing Tripura’s rich heritage and culture.
