Singda Dam

Kangpokpi district
Singda Dam is a multipurpose project on the Singda River, located about 16–20 km west of Imphal in Manipur. It is recognized as the world’s highest mud dam, standing at 920 meters above sea level. Surrounded by rice fields and lush hills, it is a popular picnic and hiking spot.
