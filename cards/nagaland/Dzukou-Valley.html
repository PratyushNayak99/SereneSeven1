Dzukou Valley

Kohima district
Dzukou Valley, located on the border of Nagaland and Manipur in Northeast India, is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, lush greenery, and rare flora like the Dzukou Lily. At an altitude of about 2,452 meters, it offers spectacular trekking trails, rolling meadows, and is a paradise for nature and adventure lovers.
